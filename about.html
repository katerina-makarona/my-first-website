About me

My Story

Hi there, I am Katerina, a UI/UX Designer with a background in Economics and experience in Digital Marketing. My journey into UI UX design started with a passion for creating intuitive, visually compelling digital experiences that bridge the gap between user needs and business goals. Coming from an analytical background and with a strong creative drive, I focus on designing interfaces that are not only aesthetically pleasing but also deeply functional and user-centred.

My experience in Digital Marketing has given me insight into understanding user behavior, while my project management and collaboration skills ensure that creative ideas come to life. When I am not designing, you will probably find me at concerts enjoying live music, at the cinema, working out, or discovering a new hobby.
